[Ternary Operator (Ternary.java)]

	A shorthand alternative to if-else statements.
	condition ? value_if_true : value_if_false;
	The ? acts like a question ("Is this true?"), and the : acts as "otherwise."
	Can return strings, integers, or doubles depending on the variable assigned.

	Activities:
		Score Evaluation (Pass/Fail)
		Check if score >= 60.
		Even or Odd Check
		Use modulus operator (%) to check for a remainder of 0.

		Check if hours < 12.
